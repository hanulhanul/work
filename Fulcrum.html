Fulcrum Analytics

Digital Design + Data Visualizations

Raw Data Analyses, Official Infographics Design & Holiday Graphic Illustration at Fulcrum Analytics

2021
Tools | Google Sheets, Adobe Illustrator, InDesign
I. CAMPAIGN INFOGRAPHIC

I analyzed raw survey data to draw meaningful points to emphasize. I organized the selected information into three sections with the respective themes of thesis, problems, and solutions. I created data-driven visualizations in correspondence to the facts. I designed infographics in three parts to respresent the analyzed data. The product was used for Fulcrum's online marketing outreach.

II. HOLIDAY GRAPHIC

I created the official holiday graphic card for 2021. The graphic has a simple storyline of creating a meaningful output relating to the feeling of anticipation during the holiday season. The desktop computer surrounded by snowy trees playfully suggests Fulcrum's continuous efforts during the holiday season. The person running the analyses is connected to the computer through various systems hanging on the holiday lights, representing Fulcrum's data-driven approaches.
